# Invoice Managerium: Stakeholder Guide 🚀

Welcome to the future of business management. **Invoice Managerium** is a premium, multi-tenant SaaS application designed to empower shop owners and streamline global business operations through a sleek, professional interface.

---

## 🌟 Core Features

### 🏢 Multi-Shop Ecosystem
Manage multiple business entities from a single unified account. Each shop operates in a secure, isolated environment, ensuring your data remains private and organized.
- **Benefit**: Scale your business empire without the administrative headache.

### 📦 Precision Inventory & Variant Support
Go beyond simple listings. Manage complex products with multiple variants (Size, Color, Material) and custom attributes.
- **Benefit**: Never lose track of stock, no matter how complex your inventory.

### 📄 Professional Instant Invoicing
Generate stunning, professional invoices in seconds. Automated calculations for discounts, shipping, and taxes ensure accuracy every time.
- **Benefit**: Build customer trust with modern, clean billing.

### 🛒 Real-Time Order Management
Track sales from creation to fulfillment. Our intuitive flow ensures that no order is left behind.
- **Benefit**: Complete visibility into your revenue streams.

### 💳 Flexible Subscription Tiers
From startups to enterprises, choose a plan that fits your current needs and upgrade effortlessly as you grow.
- **Benefit**: Pay only for what you use, when you need it.

## 👥 User Roles & Journeys

### 🛠 Super Admin (The Platform Admin)
*The Guardian of the Ecosystem.*
- **Responsibilities**: Selling and managing subscriptions, onboarding new shop owners, and overseeing platform-wide health.
- **Flow**: Manage Platform ➡️ Create Plans ➡️ Support Owners ➡️ Monitor Success.

### 👑 Shop Owner (The Business Leader)
*The Driver of Commerce.*
- **Responsibilities**: Setting up the digital storefront, selecting plans, and managing the team.
- **Flow**: Register ➡️ Create Shop ➡️ Select Plan ➡️ Invite Staff ➡️ Scale Business.

### ⚡ Moderator (The Shop Operator)
*The Engine of Daily Operations.*
- **Responsibilities**: Daily interactions—adding products, generating invoices, and fulfilling customer orders.
- **Flow**: Accept Invite ➡️ Update Inventory ➡️ Create Invoices ➡️ Fulfill Orders.
